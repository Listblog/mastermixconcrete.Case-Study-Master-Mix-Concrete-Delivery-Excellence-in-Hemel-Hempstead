Case Study: Master Mix Concrete Delivery Excellence in Hemel Hempstead

Summary

Master Mix Concrete, a trusted supplier of ‘ready mix concrete’, provided professional service in a recent project involving the pumping of concrete foundations in Hemel Hempstead. This case study explicates the detailed process, underlying the brand’s commitment to customer service as well as maintaining excellence and efficiency in delivering expert-devised ready mix concrete across suburban and city locations.

Job Background

The setting for this project was a bustling and vibrant redevelopment site of a property in Hemel Hempstead, where Master Mix Concrete was invited to return to execute the task. The busy street location presented added complexities—it required strategic planning to circumnavigate the inevitable traffic disruptions, especially during high traffic periods.

Requirements

The project was demanding, requiring intricate work. It involved the laying of foundations for a double-story house. The technical task demanded a C35 grade of ready mix concrete, with over 40m3 required, to be delivered via a line pump. The high-performance concrete ensured durability for the substantial construction.

Challenges and Solutions

Growing acquainted with the daily hustle of the Hemel Hempstead area, we recognised the many susceptibility factors. Rush hour traffic is tricky to navigate when adhering to strict deadlines, acting as a major obstacle and bringing logistical complexities.
